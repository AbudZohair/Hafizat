6 Ways You Will Be Transformed

1. Make a Roadmap to Your Goal

This program will give you the clarity to know where you are and where you want to be while providing you with the actionable steps and the tools to get there.

2. You’ll Be Committed to Your Goals

As you go through the program, you’ll find yourself more and more dedicated to your spiritual growth. You will build the habit of consistency to pursue and achieve your goals on a massive scale.

3. Change your approach

The day-by-day coaching you’re about to experience in this course is scientifically designed to deliver a small dose of information that forces your brain to think differently and to remember what you’ve learned. You’ll never think it’s too hard again.

4. Correct you Makharij

As you go through the course, you’ll be surprised by your innate

capacity to learn. We will help you read like native and eliminate mistakes. What’s more surprising is that it lasts long after you complete the course. You’ll learn rules that stick, because you’ve trained yourself to automatically repeat these habits over time.

5. Learn Tajweed Rules.

Because of the improvements, you made during this program, you’ll be ready to start learning Tajweed, to enhance your recitation. The result of this step is lasting quality of reading over a longer amount of time.

6. Start Reciting Like a Hafiz

As you correct your pronunciation, tajweed rules and daily habits to boost your productivity and focus, you’ll find yourself reading more, quality recitation in far less time than it used to take you. It’s as if you have super powers.
